Fire Facts That Need Your Attention

There were 160,367 fires in the UK in 2021. Of these fires, 30,479 were in homes.

- Fire and Rescue Services National Statistics 2021

The most common cause of fires in homes was cooking, accounting for 43% of fires. Other common causes of fires in homes included electrical faults (18%), cigarettes (13%), and children playing with matches (7%).

- Fire and Rescue Services National Statistics 2021

Cooking appliances are the leading cause of house fires, followed by smokers' materials and electrical faults.
