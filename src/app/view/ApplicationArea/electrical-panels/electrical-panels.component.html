Fire Solutions for

Electrical Panels

Short circuit in electrical panels is often the leading cause of fatal fires in buildings. Safeguarding these highly vulnerable risk points mean safeguarding the entire premises. Ceasefire's specialised fire systems are designed considering how dangerous it gets to arrest fires in these electrically charged spaces and how manual firefighting is often never an option when it comes to dousing fire in electrical panels.
